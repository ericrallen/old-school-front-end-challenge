  1. Vegetarian Cornbread Stuffing

    Prep Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: serves 10 adults

    This is a vegetarian version of my grandma's cornbread stuffing recipe. Enjoyed at Thanksgiving for decades.

    Protip: This recipe works best if you make the cornbread the night before and let it sit in the refrigerator overnight.

    Ingredients

    • I prefer the Trader Joe's brand with the little bits of corn in it, but my grandmother swore by Jiffy. The vegetarian Jiffy mix isn't as good as the omnivore one, though.

    • I like to make my own with thin strips of tempeh marinated in coconut aminos and smokey bbq suace with some smoked salt and chipotle chili powder on top.

    Instructions

    1. Use the instructions for a sheet pan to make a "Johnny Cake" instead of muffins.
